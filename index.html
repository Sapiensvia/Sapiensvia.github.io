Biography

I am Amir, born in Tabriz, now dwelling beneath the vaulted skies of Turin. A scholar of social innovation, an English teacher by twilight, and an aesthete of the European intellectual tradition. With Bach and Vivaldi in my veins and Nightwish in my soul, I seek to teach and inspire through elegance, precision, and fire.

Teaching Philosophy

My pedagogical style is Socratic in spirit, Gothic in texture, and grounded in reason. I guide students not through indoctrination but illumination—inviting them into the grand ballroom of ideas, where logic waltzes with imagination.
